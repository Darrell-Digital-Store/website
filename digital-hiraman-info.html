DIGITAL HIRAMAN

What is Digital Hiraman?

Digital Hiraman is a rental service of digital games for ps4 and ps5 at an affordable rate, making it accessible for everyone to enjoy and make the most out of their experience.

Is it safe for my account or console?

Yes, game sharing is safe since PS3 Days.

How it works?

We'll send you an account with the game that you want to play, then you'll set it as primary so you can play the game on your personal account. If you're interested, just message us so we can send you the full details and tutorial.

What is the difference between With Trophies and Without Trophies?

If your subscription is with trophies, you can play the game you rented from us on your personal account. If your subscription is without trophies, you’ll play the game you rented from us on the account that we’ll provide. Also, you need a wifi connection.

How much per rent?

It depends to the game and subscription plan.

How to rent?

Choose the game/s that you want to rent from us and then send us a message so we can assist you.

Should I pay first before I get my renting subscription?

Yes, you need to pay first if you're a new renter. Additionally, you need to pay an extra P200 for the assurance fee. However, the assurance fee is refundable at the end of your renting plan. We request an assurance fee from new customers to ensure that they will return the account after their renting plan
